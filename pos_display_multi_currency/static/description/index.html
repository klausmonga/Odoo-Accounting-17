Detailed Features List

Below is the list of features of POS Display Currency In Multi Currency

Display Payment Conversion

  • Automatic Calculations of default and desired currencies.
  • It displays both the base currency and the selected currency amount.

Option for Various Currencies

  • Allow users to create many currencies for targeting several countries' audiences.
  • It simplifies International transactions in your POS.

Currency Printed on Bills

  • The receipt displays the default and customer currency.
  • The dual display builds trust between the customer and the POS user.
  • It makes them more comfortable while making purchasing decisions.

Single Bill, Multi-Currency Option

  • Display more than one currency on a single order (default and selected currency).

Transparent Currency Conversion

  • It displays the conversion rate on the product, orderline, order total, payment line, and payment status.
  • It establishes trust and boosts accuracy, minimizing disputes.
  • Accounting occurs in the default currency only.

Session Close Popup

  • The module displays the total currencies collected during the session.
  • The Odoo app helps businesses see all collected currencies in a session.
  • It streamlines the process of reconciling accounts and ensures accurate reporting.

Pay in a Variety of Currencies per Bill

Odoo POS multi currency enables businesses to reach customers globally, pushing beyond geographical boundaries. They can pay in many currencies per bill, making it easier to remove any excess foreign currency. It not only makes transactions easy but also enhances customer relations.

Simple Order Processing

POS users are allowed to process orders in the customer's desired currency configured in the backend of odoo. It enhances convenience and streamlines the payment process. The seamless integration contributes to a sense of connection. Also, it eliminates the need for manual calculations.

Display of Currency in Bills

Displaying the currencies on your customer's bill clarifies and improves the accuracy of transactions. It helps them understand what they paid and makes the process smoother on Odoo. Displaying the local currency helps attract more customers and avoid unnecessary confusion while payment. It helps them understand their expenses.

Seamless Forex Tracking

It ensures exact financial management while providing service to customers. This feature eliminates the need for manual calculations and reduces errors. Additionally, it helps them run their operations efficiently.
